The applicant's legal classification determines what information will be required on subsequent screens. For example, an applicant with a legal classification of "Individual" will be required to enter information that is different from the information that an applicant with a legal classification of "Corporation" or "Limited Liability Company" must enter.

You must select one classification type for the applicant from the list of legal classification choices on this page. If you select "Other" for the applicant's legal classification, a text box will open in which you must describe the applicant's legal classification. If you do not choose a legal classification, you will receive an error message and will be unable to continue to the next page of the application.

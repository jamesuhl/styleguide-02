The data entry fields for Applicant Name screen depend on which legal classification you specified on the Applicant Legal Classification screen. The name you enter will be used as the Bidder Name during the auction process. For details about the type of application information that should be entered on this page of your application, click on the applicable Legal Classification type from the list below:

  • Consortium
  • Corporation
  • General Partnership
  • Government Entity
  • Individual
  • Limited Liability Company
  • Limited Liability Partnership
  • Limited Partnership
  • Trust
  • Unincorporated Association
  • Other

Consortium

On the Applicant Name/Applicant Address screen, the data entry fields for Applicant Name depend on which Legal Classification you specified on the Applicant Legal Classification screen. In this case, you are filing as a Consortium, so the system is asking for Entity Name and Jurisdiction of Formation. The name you enter will be used as the Bidder Name for purposes of the auction process. For a Consortium, Jurisdiction of Formation refers to the state under whose laws the Consortium was formed/established.

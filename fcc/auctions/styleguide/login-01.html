The Applicant Access section is password protected. Please type your FCC Registration Number (FRN) and its associated password to access the Auction Application system. An FRN/password is not required for the Public Access section of the system.

Forgot your Password?
If you forgot your FRN Password, you can contact the FRN Help Line at 877-480-3201, option 1 (Mon-Fri 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. ET). You can also email the FRN Help desk with your questions and concerns.
